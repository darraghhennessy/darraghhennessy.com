My Music

In 2019 I released my debut album, Duchenne Smile. This project featured me on Hammond Organ with Julien Colarossi on guitar and Gonzalo del Val on drums. With this album I was trying to capture the vibe of a live concert. Everything was done in one take, with everyone playing in the same live room. With the exception of the old standard "Make Someone Happy", all tracks were written and arranged by me.

During the Covid-19 lockdown in 2020, I worked on a small self-recorded project from my own home. The concept was to overdub piano and organ to create the impression of two musicians jamming with each other. This project was kindly funded by the Arts Council.
